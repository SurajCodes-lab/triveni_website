## Image Generation Tips

### General Guidelines:
1. **Use these prompts with**: Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Stable Diffusion XL, Leonardo AI, or other AI image generators
2. **Recommended settings**:
   - Aspect ratio: 16:9 for hero images, 4:3 for place images
   - Quality: High/Ultra (8k)
   - Style: Photorealistic, architectural photography
3. **Add style modifiers** as needed:
   - "professional photography"
   - "National Geographic style"
   - "cinematic lighting"
   - "vibrant colors"

### Best Practices:
- Generate multiple variations of each prompt
- Try different times of day (golden hour, blue hour, midday)
- Experiment with different weather conditions
- Consider seasonal variations (especially for hill stations)
- Ensure cultural authenticity and accuracy

### File Naming Convention:
- Hero images: `{city-slug}-hero.jpg` (e.g., `delhi-full-day-city-tour-hero.jpg`)
- Place images: `{city-slug}-{place-number}.jpg` (e.g., `delhi-full-day-city-tour-1.jpg`)
